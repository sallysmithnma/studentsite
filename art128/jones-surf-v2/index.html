legacy

John Jones grew up an avid surfer in Honolulu, HI during the 1970’s. He began his passion of shaping boards after attending high school, designing the bird logo. John has passed his passion for surfing to his four children; Malia, Mikala, Daniel and Keoni, who have all the made their mark in the surfing community.
